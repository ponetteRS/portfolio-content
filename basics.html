Basics

I like to use two cleansers. The reason that I like to use two cleansers is because I find that oftentimes I’ll have either makeup or sunscreen or other stuff that I want to get off of my face that won’t come off the first time around. So what I like to do is first use an oil cleanser and I do that because oils will help takeoff other oils from the face. And I feel much cleaner when I am able to do that. Additionally I feel like I’m able to feel a little moisturized and I find that I don’t feel my skin tighten when I use oil cleansers versus micellar water or make up remover. One thing to note is that I typically only wear lipstick if I do wear makeup so this might not work for everyone.

The next thing I like to do is use a foaming cleanser. This is because I feel like an oil cleanser always leaves an oily residue on my skin afterwards. I feel like I get that nice dry clean feeling from a foaming cleanser. One thing that I look for in a foaming cleanser is that it has salicylic acid and My reasoning for that is that I want to prevent acne. This is because my skin is super oily and I feel that when I do use salicylic acid my skin looks better and I just feel like it gives me a better feeling knowing that I have that extra protection going for me. That being said, different skin types might need more or less additives in their cleanser.

I end my routine with moisturizer. And this is because washing my face as much as I do mean that I end up having dryer skin. Even though I said I had oily skin I’ve realized over time that my skin is less oily when I put moisturizer on then when I don’t. Because of this I make sure to always wear moisturizer after I finish my routine.
